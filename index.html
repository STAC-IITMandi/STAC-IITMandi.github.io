What we do as a Club!

STAC people majorly do 4 different things. All the things are much exciting and need immense knowledge of the field.

  • Astrophysics

    Core Astrophyisics and related Mathematics, from Black holes to String Theory, from Expanding Universe to BigCrunch, we do everything…

  • Astronomy

    Sky gazing, Messier Hunts, Sky Reading and what not! From studying Stellar evolution to operating telescopes, visiting observatories…

  • Space Technology

    Working on Radio Telescope, Making Machine Learning ALgorithms and Developing Android Apps. The omain is really big which even includes automating the telescope…

  • AstroPhotography

    Having the privilege of living in a very serene place with amaxing sky, some members are over-enthusiastic for capturing the night sky in the Cameras…
